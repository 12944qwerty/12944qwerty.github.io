Although people on the receiving end may view this as a weakness, I believe it to be a strength. Just at times where the sadness isn’t as great to keep everyone happy. I don’t like failure. I really don’t like it. I’m kind of a perfectionist and I don’t like it if something doesn’t go the way I expect. That’s also a reason why I’m closed off a bit. Thankfully, my parents have tried to help me as much as they could and I’m getting better at interacting with new people. But I’m trying to get past the block everyday.
Weakness - Time Management
I absolutely suck at time management. Even though I always get my work done completely, it takes hours sometimes. However, I always complete my work when I need to.
